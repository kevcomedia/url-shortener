URL Shortener Microservice

Usage

Type /new/<http://yoururl.com> to get a shortened URL.

Examples

Click on a link below. You should see the shortened URL. Visiting that will redirect you to the original URL.

  • /new/https://duckduckgo.com
  • /new/https://khanacademy.org
  • /new/https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Randomness

Or type your URL in the field below.
